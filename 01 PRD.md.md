1. **Project Title**
	Vinyl Album Discovery Tool
2. **One Sentence Pitch**
	A curated web tool that helps vinyl listeners discover albums based on _listening context_ (mood, length, side-to-side flow) rather than genre, and links out to streaming previews and marketplace listings.
3. **Target User**
	The primary user is a casual-to-intermediate vinyl collector, roughly 16 to 35 years old, who:
	- Owns a turntable and buys records occasionally (not a die-hard collector).
	- People seeking for vinyl suggestions
	- People who just bought/receive a vinyl record player as a gift.
4. **My MVP**
	I'll tag 10 albums and categorize them into different moods, when user request for a tag, all the albums in this tag will print out.
5. **Three Must-Have Features**
	- Filtered Album Browser
	- Album Detail View 
	- Test play API, Shopping link
